Visual Timer App
I developed this app to support students with special needs. Having worked with individuals with ADHD and those on the autism spectrum, I recognized the importance of visual aids in enhancing time management and understanding. The Visual Timer app provides a clear, visual representation of time, helping users manage tasks and transitions more effectively. This project not only allowed me to apply my technical skills in both the frontend and backend, but it also gave me the opportunity to create a tool with a positive impact on users' lives.


News Aggregator App
As someone who loves staying updated with current events, I observed that many news websites lack commenting sections, which limits discussion on important topics. This inspired me to create the News Aggregator app, which compiles news stories from various sources and enables users to comment on articles that don’t offer native commenting features. This project highlights my ability to integrate multiple APIs and present data in a user-friendly way. I took full ownership of the development process, leveraging resources like instructional videos, documentation, Stack Overflow, and Reddit to resolve challenges. This project reflects my dedication to enhancing user engagement with news content while furthering my technical expertise.
